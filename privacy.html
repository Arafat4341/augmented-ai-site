When you connect your Facebook Page to Muin AI Assistant, we may collect and process the following information:

We use the collected data to:

All data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored securely with restricted access. We retain your Page data only while your Facebook Page remains connected to Muin. If you disconnect your Page, associated data is deleted from our systems.
