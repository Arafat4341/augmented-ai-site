Muin AI Assistant

Muin is an AI‑powered assistant designed to automate Facebook Page messaging, product inquiries, inventory lookups, and order management. It integrates seamlessly with your Facebook Page to provide fast, accurate, and personalized responses to your customers.

Key Features

  • AI‑generated replies to customer messages
  • Real‑time inventory lookup and product suggestions
  • Automatic order creation and tracking
  • Facebook Page integration using Meta APIs
  • Dashboard for managing chats, products, and orders
